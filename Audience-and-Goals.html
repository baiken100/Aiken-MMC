Audience and Goals

After I gathered enough research for my clients, I organized a few main goals with the intended audience.

When organizing a few main goals and audiences I needed to be as effective as possible. I started by thinking of broad topics and then focusing down to a main specific goal or audience.

I decided to lay out some overarching goals for my client, audience, and website

Client

  • Raise awareness about who they are and what they do
  • Spread the gospel
  • Educate their community
  • Educate on healthcare history in Cambodia
  • Raise life Expectancy

    • Audience

  • Aware of MMC and their services for the community
  • Educate on their healthcare option
  • Know what MMC is doing in their community
  • Know how to dontate to MMC to help fund

    • Website

  • Make it easy to donate money
  • Information is quick to access
  • Website is written also in Khmer for those who dont speak English
  • Provide accurate and up to date health care information

    • Specific Goals

    User Goal Content/Action
    Staff Help each other spread the gospel to their patients and surrounding community Pray over each patient during care, pray with families, and provide a bible in each room.
    Family Members To raise life expectancy. Raise awareness and education on preventable illnesses.
    Government Officials Educate why healthcare is so imortant to Cambodia. Raise money for better health care for citizens.
    Other Medical Professionals Provide the best medical servies possible Provide accurate and up to date health care information.
    Other Hospitals Aid other hospitals in neighboring communities. Volunteer work or interchange of patients.
    School and Tourists Educate on healthcare Take appropriate action to protect children and help the correct way through donations
