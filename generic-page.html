Design Challenge

I used a Human Centered Design worksheet provided by IDEO to aid me in determining the root problem.

    .

Frame it as a design question.

How does MMC website cater to their community?

Now, state the impact you're trying to have.

Increase knowledge of who MMC is and what they do in the community.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

I would utilize web page to the fullest, written in both English and Khmer.I would update the site to cater to all devices.

Finally, write down some of the content and constraints that you're facing.

I do not know the Khmer language, I do not know that even half of the community has access to a computer, who was the site intended for, how are they supposed to use it?

Does your origignal question need a tweak? Try it again.

How can I make the site usable for the community of Krong Ta Khmau, how can I make it more impactful for the community and MMC?
