Call To Action

After the goal was set I determined what the call to action would be. This helped determine how the site would be built.

When my research and interviews were over, I had gathered enough information to decide between two to three different "call to actions". Ultimatley I decded to focus on one main CTA which is donations.I learned through my interviews with the staff at MMC that 70% of the donations that they reciece they recieve from in country and the rest come form out of country. This information helped me udnerstand the target audience that I am reaching.
